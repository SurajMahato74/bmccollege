OUR STORY

Founded in 1997, Birat Multiple College is a renowned institution offering diverse academic programs in Biratnaagr.

1997

"Established in 1997 in the vibrant city of Biratnagar, Birat Multiple College began its journey under the Arniko Foundation with a commitment to providing quality education. Offering diverse programs in Microbiology, BSc CSE IT, and Food Technology, the college is affiliated with Tribhuvan University and has grown to become a center of academic excellence, nurturing students for success in their chosen fields."

Now

"Birat Multiple College, is now a thriving institution in Biratnagar, offering specialized programs in Microbiology, BSc CSE IT, and Food Technology. Affiliated with Tribhuvan University and operating under the Arniko Foundation, the college has continually expanded its resources and infrastructure to provide a modern and comprehensive learning environment. As our student body grows, we remain committed to fostering academic excellence and preparing students for success in their careers."

OUR CAMPUS

At Birat Multiple College, we provide a dynamic learning environment with state-of-the-art classrooms and well-equipped laboratories, ensuring students gain hands-on, job-oriented experience. Our comprehensive library offers a vast range of resources to support academic research and learning. Emphasizing practical, experience-based education, our faculty, with extensive industry expertise, guide students to develop the skills necessary for success in their careers.
